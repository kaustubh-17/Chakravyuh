F.A.Q.

  • I don't know much about quant and coding can I participate?

    Of course you can. CES organizes these events just for you to participate and improve your skills.

  • What is the importance of quant?

    Quantitative aptitude basically implies the capacity of a person to comprehend mathematical and numerical count and it's significant on the grounds that an individual with quantitative fitness will be in a superior situation to investigate and sort out the information given. It's the most important part of any recruitment process, specially in IT field.

  • Is there any entry fee to be part of this contest?

    NO. The entry is free of any cost.

  • What is the format of the event, computer-based or paper-based?

    Computer based if the event is held online, and paper based if it's conducted in campus premises. Format of second round will be a disclosed later.

  • Why we should participate in this event?

    It improves our quant and coding skills. If you are willing to improve your coding, then you can join this event.

  • Which language should I use for coding?

    You can begin with any language but the preferred languages is C/C++ and Java. These all languages are easy to learn and very fast to execute.

  • Can I submit more than one time for online rounds?

    Yes, you can, no matter how many times you submit.
